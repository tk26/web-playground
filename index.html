Why Open PID?

An important aspect of open science is the exchange of data assets or 'digital objects', such as publications, software, protocols, genome sequences

What is Open PID

OpenPID is a lightweight application that helps you to use the power of Web3 to store your digital assets on the permanent web, in three steps:

  • Your files or folders are stored on IPFS
  • The metadata about your digital asset is created in JSON-LD using [schema.org](http://schema.org/) and also stored on IPFS
  • A globally unique, persistent and resolvable identifier ('PID' or 'GUPRI') that points to both your files and your metadata is minted on ENS and permanently stored on the Ethereum blockchain
